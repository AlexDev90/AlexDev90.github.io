Who We Are

Born and brewed in Toronto, Canada since 2012, Bear Coffee & Tea has always been passionate about connecting loyal customers with carefully handcrafted products.

Our mission is to find and create a special drink for every customer. At Bear Coffee & Tea, we believe each and every coffee is unique. Different varietals of coffee, growing regions, growing conditions, bean densities & moisture content all contribute different taste character to a coffee and require different roasting profiles. Our team roast with the end goal of unlocking all of each coffee's full potential.
